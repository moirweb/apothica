How would utilizing a pharmacist benefit my practice?

Improve Patient Adherence

Did you know that 50% of patients do not take their medications as prescribed? Not taking medications correctly can lead to therapy being ineffective. A pharmacist's expertise in medications can be used to maximize therapeutic benefit, minimize side effects, and avoid drug interactions.

CUSTOMIZED TREATMENT PLAN RECOMMENDATIONS

We recognize that each patient may have different obstacles to overcome on their unique health journey. Pharmacists have access to professional tools and resources to individualize each care plan to fit the patient's needs. The pharmacist can collaborate on a care plan with the provider, and once approved, can provide an individualized consultation to the patient so that the patients themselves fully understand their plan.

FOLLOW-UP AND MONITORING

Patients may take different approaches to managing their own health based on their background, behaviors, and beliefs. Our priority is to support the unique needs of each individual and to encourage patients with a practical and achievable plan which will lead to better health outcomes. Additionally, we will follow up with the patient regularly to ensure that their plan is still safe and effective for them, and make any changes to optimize it if an opportunity exists.

OUR PROCESS

All of our pharmacist services are based off of a practiced formula to provide the patient and their healthcare providers with optimal results.

1

MEDICATION REVIEW

Our pharmacist-patient phone consults begin with a thorough review of their medication list, including prescription, over-the-counter, vitamins, and supplements. We also obtain any updates in allergies and adverse reaction history, as well as updating their immunization history and making recommendations if needed.

2

PREVENTATIVE CARE

We utilize comprehensive professional databases to ensure that each patient's therapy regimen coincides with the most recent guidelines for preventative care with considerations to their age, biometrics, and health conditions.

3

HABITS & LIFESTYLE

We embrace having a holistic perspective on each patient's health by assessing their motivation for implementing lifestyle changes. The pharmacist can then make lifestyle recommendations, which may be used alongside their medication therapy to provide improved health outcomes.

4

CARE PLAN & FOLLOW-UP

We work with each patient to create a personalized care plan and offer any additional support that they may need to help them achieve the best possible health and quality of life. We will also provide the patient with a list follow-up appointments for each provider to ensure continuity of care.
